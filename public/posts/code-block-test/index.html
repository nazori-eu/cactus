Ichiran Ramen Recipe

Introduction

Ichiran Ramen is a popular Japanese dish known for its rich broth, tender chashu, and flavorful noodles. This recipe includes all the essential components to recreate this iconic ramen experience at home.

Ingredients

Broth

  • Pork Spine (2kg): Provides a rich and hearty base.
  • Water (2kg): To make the stock.

Noodles

  • Baking Flour (300g): Forms the noodle dough.
  • Water (90g): To hydrate the flour.
  • Salt (3g): For seasoning the dough.
  • Baking Soda (4g): To give the noodles their signature texture.

Tare

  • Konbu (15g): Seaweed adds depth.
  • Dried Sardine (20g): Adds umami.
  • Bonito (Katsuobushi) (20g): A smoky fish flavor.
  • Shiitake Mushroom (10g): For earthy undertones.
  • Light Soy Sauce (300ml): Usukuchi shoyu, light and salty.
  • Salt (12g): To season the tare.
  • Sugar (5g): Balances the flavors.

Chashu

  • Pork Shoulder: Tender cuts for slow braising.
  • Soy Sauce (500ml): For marinating the meat.
  • Water (100ml): To dilute the sauce.
  • Sake (100ml): Enhances the marinade.
  • Sugar (80g): Sweetens the marinade.
  • Garlic & Ginger: Aromatics for depth of flavor.

Secret Red Sauce

  • Shaoxing Sake (25ml): Adds complexity.
  • Mirin (25ml): A touch of sweetness.
  • Cayenne Pepper Powder (8g): For a spicy kick.
  • Cooking Oil (100ml): Base for the sauce.
  • Sesame Oil (25ml): For fragrance and depth.
  • Chili Bean Sauce (1 tbsp): Adds umami and spice.
  • Garlic & Ginger: For extra aromatic layers.
  • Honey (50g): Sweetens the sauce.

Conclusion

By following these ingredient proportions, you can create a delicious bowl of Ichiran-style ramen with authentic flavors. Perfect for a comforting meal!
